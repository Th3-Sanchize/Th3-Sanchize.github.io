Our window tint films block up to 99% of harmful UV rays, protecting your skin and preventing your car's interior from fading. By investing in our UV protective window tints, you are not only safeguarding your vehicle's interior but also protecting yourself and your passengers from the harmful effects of UV radiation, such as skin cancer and premature aging.

Can I wash my car after getting it tinted?

We recommend waiting at least 48 hours before washing your car to allow the tint to fully cure. During this time, avoid rolling down the windows or using any harsh chemicals on the tinted surfaces. This waiting period helps ensure that the film adheres properly and lasts longer, providing optimal performance and protection for your vehicle.

What should I do if I see bubbles in the tint?

It is normal to see small bubbles initially, and they should disappear within a few days as the tint cures.
