Frequently Asked Questions

What are the benefits of window tinting?

Window tinting provides UV protection, reduces glare, enhances privacy, and improves the overall look of your vehicle.

How long does the tinting process take?

The tinting process typically takes 1-2 hours depending on the size and type of vehicle.

Is the tinting film guaranteed?

Yes, we offer a warranty on all our tinting films to ensure quality and customer satisfaction.

Can I wash my car after getting it tinted?

We recommend waiting at least 48 hours before washing your car to allow the tint to fully cure.

What should I do if I see bubbles in the tint?

It is normal to see small bubbles initially, and they should disappear within a few days. If they persist, contact us for assistance.
