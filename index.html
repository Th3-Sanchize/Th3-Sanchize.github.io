About Us

Welcome to Chroma Culture Tint, where we bring the shade to you! Our mobile tinting service means we come to your location, but you can also drop off your vehicle at our place if that's more convenient. Our mission? To enhance your driving experience with style, privacy, and protection. With years of expertise and a team that's as friendly as a summer breeze, we guarantee top-notch service with a smile (and maybe a joke or two).

Why choose us? Because we believe that every car deserves to look and feel its best. Whether you're cruising downtown or parked at the beach, our tints offer superior UV protection, heat reduction, and an extra layer of privacy. So, sit back, relax, and let us do the shading!

Our Services

Bronze Package

Our entry-level offering featuring basic dyed film. It's affordable, looks good, and does the job of reducing glare and heat. Perfect for those who want to tint on a budget. Hey, even bronze shines in the right light!

  • Reduces glare and heat
  • Cost-effective solution
  • Great for privacy

Silver Package

Step up your game with our Carbon window tint. This middle-tier option offers better heat rejection and a sleek, matte finish. Ideal for those who want more than just the basics. Because who doesn't want to be a silver fox on the road?

  • Superior heat rejection
  • Non-reflective, matte finish
  • Enhanced durability

Gold Package

Go for gold with our top-tier Ceramic window tint. This package offers the best in UV protection, heat rejection, and clarity. Plus, it looks stunning. For those who want the best and aren't afraid to show it.

  • Maximum UV protection
  • Exceptional heat rejection
  • Crystal clear clarity
